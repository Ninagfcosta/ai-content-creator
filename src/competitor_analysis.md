# Competitor Analysis — Productivity App Market 2025

## Main Competitors

### 1. Notion
- **Strengths:** Highly flexible, large community, template library
- **Weaknesses:** Too complex for new users, steep learning curve
- **Price:** Free tier + $10/month Pro
- **AI Features:** Basic AI writing assistant (bolted on later)
- **Gap:** Not designed for simplicity or everyday users

### 2. Todoist
- **Strengths:** Simple task management, clean interface
- **Weaknesses:** No AI features, limited collaboration tools
- **Price:** Free tier + $4/month Pro
- **AI Features:** None native
- **Gap:** No AI integration, no ecosystem

### 3. Monday.com
- **Strengths:** Enterprise features, strong team management
- **Weaknesses:** Expensive, complex, overkill for individuals
- **Price:** $9-19/month per user
- **AI Features:** Limited automation
- **Gap:** Not accessible for small teams or individuals

### 4. Asana
- **Strengths:** Good for teams, project tracking
- **Weaknesses:** Poor mobile experience, expensive
- **Price:** Free tier + $10.99/month Premium
- **AI Features:** Basic automation only
- **Gap:** Not AI-native, poor for personal productivity
